BEBASIA Smart Fingerprint Door Lock

Elevate your home security with the BEBASIA smart fingerprint door lock. This advanced lock combines convenience with top-tier protection, offering multiple access methods such as fingerprint, app, web portal, key cards, passcode, and even eKey sharing. Perfect for modern homes, it allows you to manage access for family, friends, or approved service providers with just a few taps on your smartphone.

The lock supports up to 50 fingerprints and features a smart timer system for easy remote access control. With built-in backup options like 2 physical keys and a thumb turn, you can rest assured that access is always available in emergencies. Additionally, it includes anti-peeping password protection and a limit on incorrect passcode attempts, ensuring peace of mind for every homeowner.

Smart Deadbolt for Apartments & More

Whether you're securing an apartment, hotel, office, or Airbnb property, the BEBASIA smart deadbolt is built to meet your needs. It offers seamless integration with Alexa or Google Home, empowering you to control your front door from anywhere. Easily customize the auto-lock timer between 5 and 900 seconds using the BEBASIA app, or use the onboard keypad by pressing '#' for 2 seconds for quick, one-touch locking.

Designed for rapid installation, the lock is ready in under 15 minutes with no extra drilling required. Its sleek satiny nickel finish matches a wide range of interior styles, and it's compatible with both left and right-handed doors, making it a versatile choice for any property.
