ENOKER Smart Deadbolt

Discover a smart door lock that combines convenience with cutting-edge security. The ENOKER Smart Deadbolt is designed for modern homes and offices, offering multiple unlock methods that make it easy to use while keeping your property safe. Whether you're using a fingerprint, entering a passcode, or controlling it via the Tuya app, this deadbolt ensures worry-free access and protection.

Advanced Security, Easy Access

With a multi-biometric system that supports fingerprints, IC cards, and app-based controls, the ENOKER Smart Deadbolt is built to meet your security needs. The built-in Tuya app allows for remote management, giving you the ability to lock or unlock your door from anywhere. This makes it an ideal solution for busy individuals and families who want smart technology that simplifies daily life.

Efficient & Reliable

Thanks to its long-lasting battery, the ENOKER Smart Deadbolt can operate for up to 12 months, reducing the need for frequent replacements. The powerful battery, combined with a back-up key and USB charging option, ensures that you're always in control. Moreover, the lock is easy to install with helpful voice guidance in both English and Chinese, making it accessible to all users.

Manage Your Doors with Confidence

From keyless entry to auto-lock functions, the ENOKER Smart Deadbolt offers a seamless experience for both residential and commercial use. It also features an anti-locked mode that prevents accidental lockouts, giving you peace of mind. Whether you're at home, at work, or away, this smart deadbolt provides smart technology that keeps your door secure and accessible with minimal effort.
